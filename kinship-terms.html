Residence, Lineage & Authority
are three different questions

Matrilocal / patrilocal, matrilineal / patrilineal, and matriarchal / patriarchal are often used interchangeably — but each pair answers a completely independent question about a family system.

Where a couple lives, whose line the children inherit through, and who holds authority can point in three different directions at once.

§2

Key differences, pair by pair

Matrilocal vs Patrilocal Residence

Matrilocal

  • Husband moves to the wife's family after marriage.
  • Concerned with place of residence.

Patrilocal

  • Wife moves to the husband's family after marriage.
  • Concerned with place of residence.
Matrilineal vs Patrilineal Lineage / Descent

Matrilineal

  • Descent, clan, and inheritance are traced through the mother.
  • Children belong to the mother's lineage.

Patrilineal

  • Descent, clan, and inheritance are traced through the father.
  • Children belong to the father's lineage.
Matriarchal vs Patriarchal Authority / Power

Matriarchal

  • Women hold authority and decision-making power.
  • Mother or senior woman is the head.

Patriarchal

  • Men hold authority and decision-making power.
  • Father or senior man is the head.
§3

Memory trick

Local = Location
Where the couple lives after marriage.
Lineal = Lineage
Through whose family descent and inheritance are traced.
Archal = Authority
Who holds power in the family or society.
§4

Worked example

Suppose Ravi marries Priya.

  • Ravi moves to Priya's houseMatrilocal
  • Priya moves to Ravi's housePatrilocal
  • Children inherit through Priya's familyMatrilineal
  • Children inherit through Ravi's familyPatrilineal
  • Priya or senior women make major decisionsMatriarchal
  • Ravi or senior men make major decisionsPatriarchal
§5

Important exam note

These concepts are independent of each other:

  • Matrilocal ≠ Matrilineal
  • Matrilineal ≠ Matriarchal
  • Patrilocal ≠ Patriarchal

A society may be:

  • Matrilocal but Patrilineal
  • Matrilineal but Patriarchal
  • Patrilocal and Patriarchal

Always identify whether the question is asking about:

  1. Residence (Local)
  2. Descent / Inheritance (Lineal)
  3. Authority / Power (Archal)
